About Me

SJ

Hello! I'm Sujal Javeri, a passionate first-year B.Tech student at KJ College of Engineering, Pune, with an entrepreneurial spirit and a vision for the future.

At just 18, I've already ventured into multiple business domains - from running Wish Vault, my Instagram-based POD apparel store, to building Innovate Together, a community startup focused on business and tech innovation.

My journey hasn't been conventional. After losing my father in 2012, I learned resilience early. Inspired by my mother who runs her own parlour business, I developed a passion for building things from scratch and turning ideas into reality.

I'm particularly fascinated by AI, Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity, and I believe these technologies will shape our future. My goal is to create impactful ventures that combine technology with real-world solutions.
